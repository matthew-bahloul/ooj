About Us

Who Are We?

The Occasional Orange Juice Drinkers maboutovement has been active since the mid 2010s.

Once a small club, the Occasional Orange Juice Drinkers, has grown into an all-purpose group where people can meet, network, and more without being judged for their orange juice preferences.

We are:

  • Inclusive of all orange juice preferences
  • Respectful of all orange juice preferences
  • Super cool as heck


The History

The Occasional Orange Juice Drinkers began in Sylmar California when a group of friends met to make orange-juice-based cocktails.

While buying orange juice and other cocktail ingredients, one of the original members, it remains unclear which one, decided to buy pears with the intenion of making a non-orange-juice-based cocktail. At that moment, the group members realized that everyone deserves respect and is valuable regardless of their orange juice preferences.
